People

Dr. Bin Sun is now an Assistant Professor and the Principal Investigator (PI) of the Research Center for Pharmacoinformatics in the College of Pharmacy, Harbin Medical University. He studied in the Huazhong University of Scienece and Technology from 2008-2015 to obtain a BSc and Master degrees in Biopharmacy and Medicinal Chemistry, respectively. After that he went to University of Kentucky to persue a doctoral degree in Computational Biochemisty with Prof. Peter M. Kekenes-Huskey from 2015-2019. After a two-years' postdoc experience in the Stritch School of Medicine, Loyola University Chcago, he obtained the current position. His research interests are to use compulational modeling techniqies (mostely molecular dynamics simulations) to "observe" how proteins and nucleic acids behave in a microscopic world, and to reveal the thermodynamics and biophysics underlying these events. While still being facinated by the endless theoretical beautify beneath the biomolecule events, he is also passionate to apply these understandings to better design drugs.
